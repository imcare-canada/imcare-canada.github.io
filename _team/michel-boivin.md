Dr. Michel Boivin received his medical degree from Mcgill University and completed internal medicine residency there. He completed a critical care fellowship at the University of New Mexico. While there Dr. Boivin underwent training in echocardiography including trans-esophageal echocardiography, and  first completed the National Board of Echocardiography examination in 2013 and again in 2023. Dr. Boivin is a well known expert in teaching advanced echocardiography and Trans-esophageal echocardiography in critical care and has presented and taught at numerous international conferences. He has numerous published  articles and book chapters related to ultrasound. Currently Dr. Boivin works as an intensive care doctor and internal medicine specialist at NHH.
